Biltmore Hotel Tbilisi

About the hotel

City-center hotel gives Tbilisi guests views of river and mountains

The stunning 5-star Biltmore Tbilisi Hotel is located in Tbilisi, only 10 minutes on foot from Rooms, and offers an indoor swimming pool. Set within 2.2 km from the ancient Narikala Fortress, it is this luxury hotel to stay in Old Tbilisi district of the city. Guests will also appreciate proximity to Freedom Square, which is nearly a 20-minute stroll away. The 32-storey upscale hotel is set in the very heart of Tbilisi. This Tbilisi property is centrally located close to Rustaveli Square. The Biltmore Tbilisi Hotel features 214 rooms equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels as well as a mini-fridge-bar, tea and coffee making facilities. They are decorated with soundproof windows along with elegant furniture. Some of these luxury units look out over the river.
