Marriott Hotel Tbilisi

About the hotel

Embrace majesty at our 5-star hotel in Tbilisi, Georgia

Nestled in a historic building along Rustaveli Avenue, Tbilisi Marriott Hotel provides guests with an enviable vantage point from which to explore Georgia's vibrant capital city. Step into our doors and take in stunning architectural details, such as an atrium-style lobby replete with striking crystal chandeliers. Settling in is easy, thanks to our artfully designed accommodations, boasting sleek marble bathrooms, majestic bedding, high-speed Wi-Fi and scenic city views. Fuel up at our two unique dining destinations; Parnas Cafe is our signature restaurant serving authentic Georgian cuisine and global favorites, while Majestic Bar & Lounge is the ideal spot for freshly-brewed coffee, sumptuous pastries and craft cocktails. Reboot with a workout in our 24-hour fitness center or plan a meeting or special event in our 4,348 square feet of modern venue space. Should you wish to explore Tbilisi, our hotel is just a few moments from attractions and landmarks like Liberty Square, Narikala Fortress and MOMA.
